About CYBERSUMIT

CYBERSUMIT is a cybersecurity learning platform created to help beginners learn Kali Linux, Linux commands, Networking, Ethical Hacking, and Cyber Security in a simple and practical way.

Our goal is to provide free tutorials, installation guides, troubleshooting tips, and educational resources for students and technology enthusiasts.

Whether you're installing Kali Linux for the first time or beginning your ethical hacking journey, CYBERSUMIT aims to make learning easier through clear, step-by-step guides.
